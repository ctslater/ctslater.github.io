Colin Slater

University of Washington
LSST Data Management

I am a postdoc at the University of Washington, working on Milky Way structure and the population of dwarf galaxies in the Local Group. This includes observing the tidal debris left behind by dwarfs as they fall onto the Galaxy, along with modeling the changing properties of dwarfs as they become satellites of the Milky Way. Much of my work uses data from the Pan-STARRS survey.

I am also the LSST Data Systems Fellow, and I support that project with analyses of the scientific requirements and expected performance of the survey.
